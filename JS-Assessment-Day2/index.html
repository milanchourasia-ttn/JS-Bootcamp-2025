Q.Explain how hoisting affects the execution of console.log(z) in second().

Due to hoisting, z is declared at the top of second function but remains undefined until it is assigned a value.

Q.Explain the scope chain for console.log(y) in second().

When console.log(y) is executed inside second function,then below steps to be follow:
1. Check in the Local Scope
2. Check in the Outer Scope
3. Check in the Global Scope

Q.What happens when console.log(y) is executed outside first() (Line 4)? Why?

ReferenceError: y is not defined - because the console.log(y); at Line 4 is trying to access y in the global scope But y is only defined inside first(), and it's not accessible outside.
